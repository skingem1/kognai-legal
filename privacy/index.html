Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026  ·  Kael by Kognai

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Kael by Kognai ("we", "the Service") handles information when you connect your TikTok account. Kael is a read-only creator intelligence tool — it reads your TikTok profile and stats only and never posts, modifies, or deletes any content.

2. Information We Access

When you connect your TikTok account via Login Kit, the Service reads the following data using TikTok's approved OAuth scopes:

  • user.info.profile: display name, username, bio description, profile link, and verification status — used to identify the connected creator account
  • user.info.stats: follower count, following count, total likes, and video count — used to compute your baseline engagement velocity score

We do not access your TikTok messages, comments, direct messages, followers list, or any data beyond the two approved scopes above.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.
  • We do not store TikTok access tokens on any remote server.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.
  • We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising on this website.
  • We do not post, upload, modify, or delete any content on your TikTok account.

4. How We Use Information

The profile and stats data accessed via TikTok Login Kit is used exclusively to:

  • Identify the authorized creator account and link it to Kognai's content scoring pipeline
  • Compute a baseline engagement velocity score (follower count, video count, likes count) refreshed every 24 hours
  • Benchmark content performance against this baseline

5. Data Storage

The Service runs locally on the authorized user's own machine. TikTok access tokens are stored only in the local environment on the user's device and are never transmitted to any external Kognai server. Stats data is stored locally in the user's own Kognai pipeline database.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with:

  • TikTok — profile and stats read via Login Kit API — governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

You may revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings under "Manage app permissions." Revoking access immediately stops any further data access by the Service.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date at the top reflects the most recent update.

10. Contact
