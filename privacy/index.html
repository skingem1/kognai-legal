Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026  ·  Kognai

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Kognai ("we", "the Service") handles information when you authorize it to post content to TikTok on your behalf.

2. Information We Access

When you connect your TikTok account, the Service accesses:

  • TikTok user identifier (open_id) — used to associate your account with the Service
  • Content posting permission — used solely to publish archive media you have authorized

We do not access your TikTok profile, followers, messages, or any other account data beyond what is required for content posting.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identifying information such as your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not store TikTok access tokens beyond active session use.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics on this website.

4. How We Use Information

The TikTok user identifier and access token are used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate API requests to TikTok on your behalf
  • Post content you have authorized to your TikTok account

5. Data Storage

The Service runs locally on the authorized user's own machine. TikTok access tokens are stored in the local environment only (`.env` file on the user's device) and are never transmitted to any Kognai server.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with:

  • TikTok (content posting via Content Posting API v2) — governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • Internet Archive (media sourcing) — governed by Internet Archive's Terms

7. Your Rights

You may revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings under "Manage app permissions". Revoking access immediately stops any further posting.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via GitHub.
